Flipkart prices were highly varied as compared to amazon.

It was generally observed that on some days Flipkart overshoots its price,and then bounces back to its original price.

This trend was also seen in amazon but the overshoot price was less as compared to flipkart.

During the festive time [20-25 October] Flipkart increased its price more as compared to amazon.

Number of user ratings were more for Amazon indicating that more people purchased from Amazon.

As conclusion we can say, Amazon is a better choice, but it is always recommended to check prices at both platforms before purchasing any product.
